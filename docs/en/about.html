About Media Manager

Media Manager is a desktop application built with PySide6 to keep movie and TV show collections organised. It combines fast local scanning with rich online metadata so that your library stays clean, searchable, and visually appealing.

Author janeT

janeT leads the product vision and authored the first release of Media Manager. Their focus on practical workflows shaped the batch tools, dashboard, and help experience you see throughout the app.

What You Can Expect

  • Multi-library support for movies and episodic content
  • Automatic metadata downloads from TMDB and TVDB
  • Poster, subtitle, and NFO generation pipelines
  • Productivity helpers such as batch actions, tags, and collections
  • Localized help content so teams can work in their preferred language
